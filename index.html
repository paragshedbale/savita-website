About the Advocate

A Legacy of Legal Excellence Across Maharashtra

Adv. Savita Shedbale-Inamdar is one of Maharashtra's most respected voices in criminal law and workplace rights — known for her tenacity in court and her compassionate counsel to every client she serves.

With over three decades of practice spanning serious criminal matters and bodily offence litigation, Adv. Shedbale-Inamdar has built a formidable reputation at the district level and at the Kolhapur Bench of the Mumbai High Court — one of Maharashtra's newest and most significant judicial forums.

Her appointment as the first female President of the Sangli Bar Association reflects the deep trust she commands across the legal fraternity. As a member of P.O.S.H committees in various institutions, she brings legal rigour and a commitment to gender justice to organisations across Maharashtra — helping them build safe, compliant workplaces under the POSH Act, 2013.

What I Offer

Services Tailored to Your Needs

Whether you are an individual seeking justice or an organisation building a safe, compliant workplace — there is a service designed for you.

01 — P.O.S.H

External IC Member Empanelment

Serve as the legally mandated External Member on your company's Internal Committee under the POSH Act, 2013. Bring legal credibility to your IC and avoid penalties of up to ₹50,000 for non-compliance.

02 — P.O.S.H

P.O.S.H Compliance Audit

Comprehensive review of your organisation's POSH policy, IC constitution, complaint procedures, and annual reporting obligations — ensuring full legal compliance and zero regulatory exposure.

03 — P.O.S.H

IC Training & Sensitisation

Structured workshops for IC members and employees on POSH Act provisions, complaint handling, inquiry procedures, and maintaining a gender-sensitive workplace culture.

04 — P.O.S.H

P.O.S.H Policy Drafting

Drafting and reviewing workplace sexual harassment prevention policies aligned with the POSH Act, 2013 — tailored to your organisation's size, sector, and specific compliance requirements.

05 — Criminal Law

Criminal Law Representation
