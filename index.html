About the Advocate

A Legacy of Legal Excellence Across Maharashtra

Adv. Savita Shedbale is one of Maharashtra's most respected voices in criminal law and workplace rights — known for her tenacity in court and her compassionate counsel to every client she serves.

With over three decades of practice spanning serious criminal matters, POCSO cases, and bodily offence litigation, Adv. Shedbale has built a formidable reputation at the district level and at the Kolhapur Bench of the Mumbai High Court — one of Maharashtra's newest and most significant judicial forums.

Her appointment as the first female President of the Sangli Bar Association reflects the deep trust she commands across the legal fraternity. Today, she channels this expertise into a focused POSH practice — serving as an External Member on Internal Committees and helping organisations across Maharashtra build legally compliant, safe workplaces.

Services Tailored to Your Needs

Whether you are an individual seeking justice or an organisation building a safe, compliant workplace — there is a service designed for you.

01 — POSH

External IC Member Empanelment

Serve as the legally mandated External Member on your company's Internal Committee. Bring legal credibility to your IC and avoid penalties of up to ₹50,000 for non-compliance.

02 — POSH

POSH Compliance Audit

Comprehensive review of your organisation's POSH policy, IC constitution, complaint procedures, and annual reporting — ensuring full legal compliance and zero regulatory exposure.

03 — POSH

IC Training & Sensitisation

Structured workshops for IC members and employees on POSH Act provisions, complaint handling, inquiry procedures, and maintaining a gender-sensitive workplace culture.

04 — POSH

POSH Policy Drafting
